Nigeria Extractive Industries Transparency Initiative (NEITI)

Approach

The Nigeria Extractive Industries Transparency Initiative (NEITI) is the national chapter of the global Extractive Industries Transparency Initiative (EITI) mandated by law (The NEITI Act 2007) to promote transparency and accountability in the management of Nigeria’s oil, gas and mining revenues. The NEITI website serves as a dynamic platform, meticulously designed to present comprehensive insights into Nigeria's oil, gas, and mining sectors. It stands as a beacon for transparency, revealing crucial information about revenue management.

The website incorporates intricate logic to interrelate various aspects, offering a nuanced perspective on the management and stakeholders involved in Nigeria's extractive industries. This includes detailed insights into the roles of different stockholders, enabling a comprehensive view of the industry landscape. Joining this transformative project, I honed my skills in problem-solving and reasoning, contributing to the creation of a platform that goes beyond data presentation, offering a deeper understanding of the complexities inherent in the oil, gas, and mining sectors.

Tech Stack

React   Material UI   Redux   Axios
